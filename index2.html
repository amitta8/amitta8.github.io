Cancer in the United States (2017)

By: Anya Mittal

Cancer has become the second leading cause of death in the world, about a fourth of US deaths are due to cancer, and there are over 100 types of cancers that exist today. Cancer cases are rising significantly and currently, there is no cure to cancer, there are only very difficult treatments. This visualization will go in depth about cancer cases by state, which types had the most cases, and which types of cancer caused the most deaths in the United States in the year 2017. The source used for this visualization is https://gis.cdc.gov/Cancer/USCS/DataViz.html
